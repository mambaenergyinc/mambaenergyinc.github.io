Our Mission

Renewable Energy sources account for a quarter of the electricity generated globally.

Mamba Energy Inc. is a group of engineers working to harness solar and wind power to power an RC Vehicle.

Our goal is to enter the American Society of Mechanical Engineers Student Design Competition, and prove we have the best design.
